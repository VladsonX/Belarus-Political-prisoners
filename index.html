How to write a letter to a political prisoner?

What is the purpose of this event?

Today, on May 21st, we stand in solidarity with Belarusian political prisoners. We, Belarusian students studying in Lublin, have found refuge far from our homeland. For many of us, this escape was prompted by urgent political persecution. Now, we strive to shed light on the plight of numerous political prisoners in Belarus.
You can learn more at the following link.

What can I do?

Currently in Belarus, there are over 1385 political prisoners, completely isolated from the outside world. These are people serving their sentences for fabricated criminal cases against them, and they are in need of our support. And there's a safe and simple way to support these individuals - send them a letter!

You can write a letter online or by hand.

What to write about?

What to write about?

You CAN write

You can write about any abstract topics! If nothing comes to mind immediately, you can write about the following:

  • How your day went?
  • Places you've visited, your hometown, favorite spots
  • What good happened?
  • Nature, weather
  • Your favorite hobbies
  • Songs, movies, theater WITHOUT political context
  • Draw something!
  • Sports activities, meditation
  • Psychological practices for self-help
  • Riddles and puzzles, jokes and anecdotes
  • Tell about your dreams and ideas
  • Any words of support and hope

You CANNOT write

Your letter cannot contain anything from the list below:

  • Personal information (about yourself and family)
  • Political topics, slogans
  • Abbreviations and foreign words
  • Insults and profanity
  • Extremist organizations
  • Metaphors and allegories
  • Criminal cases
  • Emojis, stickers
  • Leaving blank sheets of paper

Example of a letter

Example of a letter

If you write a handwritten letter

If you want to write a handwritten letter, you should write in Russian or Belarusian!
Also don't hesitate to reach out to the event organizers for assistance.

Take a postcard if you are not expecting a response.
Take a reply card if you are expecting a response and have a return Belarusian address. If you want to receive a response but do not have a Belarusian address, leave your name, surname, and email separately on the paper so that we can identify the response and send it to you.

If you write a letter online

If you want to write a letter online, you can do it in any convenient language using the Google Form. The letter will be translated by us and sent to the political prisoner.
